Home Illustrations Paintings Renders
  1. Illustrations

    2. Illustrations consist of art work that have an outline as well as full color and shaded detail.

  2. Paintings

    3. Paintings are a type of art work that can start with a sketch but have no visable outline and have alot of brush stroke detail creating a realistic feel to scene and characters in the image.

  3. Renders

    4. Renders are 3d images rendered in Blender which is a free 3d software program that helps to learn about 3d modeling,and many styles of images can be made in blender.
